# BWE 90-Day Growth Plan

## Weeks 1-2 (Foundation)

- Finalize event taxonomy + KPI dashboard spec
- Launch referral primitives (codes, invite endpoint, attribution)
- Launch creator/ambassador intake + tracking links
- Owner: Product + Eng + Growth

## Weeks 3-4 (Activation)

- Business profile share kit + QR
- Seller onboarding optimization (time-to-first-product)
- Employer/talent intake pipeline instrumentation
- Owner: Eng + Ops

## Weeks 5-6 (Distribution)

- City/category wedge campaign v1
- Creator campaign calendar starts
- Review request flow for directory + marketplace
- Owner: Growth + Community

## Weeks 7-8 (Conversion)

- Referral rewards v1
- Sponsored/featured upsell path optimization
- Jobs/employer funnel conversion improvements
- Owner: Growth + Monetization

## Weeks 9-10 (Retention)

- Repeat buyer nudges (wishlist + follow seller)
- Business follow-up prompts (share profile, request reviews)
- Talent spotlight cadence
- Owner: Product + Ops

## Weeks 11-12 (Scale decision)

- Evaluate wedge KPIs and decide expand/iterate
- Document replication plan for second city/category
- Owner: Leadership + Growth

## KPI targets (90-day)

- +25% visitor->signup
- +20% signup->first-action
- referral share >= 15% of new signups
- seller time-to-first-order down 30%
- employer repeat post rate +20%

## Dependencies

- stable runtime and route reliability
- event tracking consistency
- creator and partner onboarding capacity
