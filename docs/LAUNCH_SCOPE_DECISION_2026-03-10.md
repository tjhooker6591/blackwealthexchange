# Launch Scope Decision — Consulting / Opportunity (2026-03-10)

## Goal
Ship production-ready-today with clear, reliable user flows and no confusing partial public lanes.

## Launch Now (active)
- `/recruiting-consulting` (employer + candidate intake)
- `/job-listings` (public browse, apply/save gated by auth where needed)
- `/post-job` (employer lane)

## Quieted for post-cutline expansion
- Internships growth lane emphasis in `/jobs` hub
- Freelance/gig lane emphasis in `/jobs` hub
- Mentorship matching lane emphasis in `/jobs` hub

These lanes remain available as future routes, but are presented as post-launch expansion items in the hub to avoid over-promising unfinished workflow depth.

## Rationale
- Keeps opportunity funnel coherent for launch: discover jobs, post jobs, submit recruiting consulting intake.
- Reduces user confusion from partially mature lanes.
- Preserves roadmap visibility without blocking production cutline.
