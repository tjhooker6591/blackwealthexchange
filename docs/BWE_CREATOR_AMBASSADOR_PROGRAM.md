# BWE Creator & Ambassador Program

## Tracks

1. HBCU Ambassadors
2. City Ambassadors
3. Creator Cohorts (category-focused)

## Recruitment criteria

- audience relevance to Black economics/lifestyle/career
- content consistency
- conversion potential (not only followers)
- trust/safety fit

## Compensation model

- tracked referral payout (tiered)
- performance bonus for GMV and verified signups
- non-cash perks (featured placement, badges)

## Campaign calendar (monthly)

- Week 1: Businesses to know
- Week 2: Shop Black weekend
- Week 3: Jobs & internships roundup
- Week 4: Founder/creator spotlight

## Required assets

- creator landing pages
- campaign codes
- share cards
- UTM/referral templates

## KPIs

- creator clicks
- creator signups
- creator-attributed GMV
- creator-attributed business/employer leads
