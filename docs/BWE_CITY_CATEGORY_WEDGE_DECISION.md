# BWE City/Category Wedge Decision Framework

## Objective
Select first wedge from platform evidence, not opinion.

## Inputs required
- current listing density by city/category
- seller readiness by category
- search demand signals (queries, CTR, conversion)
- creator availability by city/category
- partnership access (HBCU/chambers/community)
- early transaction likelihood

## Scoring model (0-5 each)
1. Supply density
2. Repeat demand potential
3. Shareability potential
4. Partnership leverage
5. Creator leverage
6. Operational simplicity
7. 60-day monetization probability

Total score determines wedge rank.

## Decision output format
- selected city cluster
- selected 1-2 categories
- selected creator cohort
- selected employer/talent wedge
- expected 30/60/90-day KPI targets

## Build-now vs build-later
### Build-now
- top wedge only
- localized pages and campaigns
- creator and partner launch in wedge
### Build-later
- second city/category expansion after wedge KPIs pass threshold

## Risks / missing data
- incomplete attribution data
- uneven listing quality by city
- partner pipeline uncertainty

## Gate to expansion
Expand only if wedge passes:
- repeat purchase threshold
- referral share threshold
- creator-attributed conversion threshold
- seller retention threshold
